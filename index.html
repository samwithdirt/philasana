Our Story

We Refused to Look Away

There was a time when the streets of Kabokweni told a story no one wanted to tell. Kids with nowhere to go. Families quietly falling apart. A community drowning in problems the world had stopped paying attention to.

We saw it. We felt it. And we decided — enough.

Not with anger. Not with politics. But with a ball, a field, and the unshakeable belief that when people come together — really come together — everything changes. One event became a movement. A movement became a family. And that family is still growing.

🔥

Our Mission

To pour purpose into every corner of our community — through sport, health and human connection — until every person knows they matter.

🌍

Our Vision

A Kabokweni — and one day a South Africa — where no child grows up believing the streets are their only option.

🤝

Our Values

Ubuntu. Showing up. Never giving up on your neighbour. That's the only rulebook we follow.

What We Do

Our Programmes & Services

From competitive sport to wellness bootcamps, Phila Sana offers impactful programmes that bring people together and build stronger communities.

⚽
Community Soccer Tournaments

Competitive football tournaments bringing together schools, businesses, SAPS, hospitals and community clubs — open to all ages including under-15 development matches.

🏐
Netball Programmes

Empowering women and girls through netball — from grassroots participation to competitive play. Building confidence, teamwork and physical wellbeing.

💪
Fitness Bootcamps

High-energy bootcamp sessions led by certified trainers from Iron Ex. Promoting active, healthy lifestyles and mental resilience in communities across Mpumalanga.

🎉
Family Fun Days

Large-scale community events featuring soccer, netball, bootcamp, jumping castles, entertainment and youth activities — free general entry for the community.

🌍
Social Development Initiatives

Tackling crime, GBV, mental health and bullying through awareness campaigns aligned with the #BeProudOfYourCommunity movement.

🏆
Recognition & Awards

Celebrating excellence through trophies, medals and public recognition — honouring athletes, volunteers and community champions.
